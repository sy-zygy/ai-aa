# Expert Advisory Council

**Owners:** Research Director (identification & recruitment) + Operations Director (governance & onboarding) | **Target:** 7-12 members by Q3 2026

The advisory council provides expert input and institutional credibility. Members advise on research priorities, review deliverables, lend their names and expertise to AI-AA's work, and connect the organization to their professional networks. The council has no governance authority — that belongs to the board of directors.

## Recruitment Process

1. **Research** identifies candidates based on expertise, publications, public positions, and network connections
2. **Research** creates a candidate profile page in this section
3. **CEO** and **Operations** review for strategic fit and conflict-of-interest concerns
4. **CEO** or warm contact makes initial outreach
5. **Operations** manages onboarding, terms of engagement, and ongoing coordination
