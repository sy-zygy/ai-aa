# Media Relations

**Owner:** Communications Director

## Media Strategy

AI-AA's media strategy starts with disability-specific media (highest credibility, most aligned audience), then expands to tech policy media, and eventually mainstream outlets for major milestones.

## Target Media by Phase

### Phase 1: Pre-Launch / Soft Launch
- Disability-specific: Disability Scoop, New Mobility, Rooted in Rights
- Accessibility-focused: WebAIM newsletter, A11y Weekly, Inclusive Design communities
- Niche tech: Assistive technology publications

### Phase 2: Public Launch
- Tech policy: Politico tech, The Hill tech, Protocol/successor
- Tech media: Wired, The Verge, Ars Technica, TechCrunch
- Education tech: EdSurge, Inside Higher Ed, Chronicle of Higher Education
- Health tech: STAT News, Health Affairs, Modern Healthcare

### Phase 3: Major Milestones
- Mainstream: NYT disability/tech beats, Washington Post, NPR, Associated Press
- Legal media: Law360, Bloomberg Law, ABA Journal
- TV/podcast: Relevant disability, tech policy, or civil rights shows
