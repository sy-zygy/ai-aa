## Content Templates

### Story Thread (X/Twitter)
```
Meet [first name]. [One-sentence situation].

Every day, [they] use AI to [specific comprehension task]. It [specific benefit].

But [institution/system] blocks AI tools on [platform/document].

So [they] can't [specific thing they can't do].

This is what happens when we don't recognize AI as an accessibility tool.

[Name]'s story is why @AIAccessAlliance exists.

Everyone deserves to understand.
```

### Policy Explainer (LinkedIn)
```
Did you know?

The ADA already requires [covered entity type] to provide "auxiliary aids and services" for effective communication.

Screen readers? Auxiliary aid.
Sign language interpreters? Auxiliary aid.
Braille displays? Auxiliary aid.

AI that helps someone understand their medical diagnosis?

[The legal argument, in 2-3 sentences.]

This is what AI Access Alliance is working to establish. [Link to doctrine summary or website.]
```

### Milestone Announcement (Both)
```
[Big news emoji-free announcement]

[What happened, in one sentence]

[Why it matters, in one sentence]

[What's next, in one sentence]

[Thank yous if applicable]
```

## Engagement Guidelines

- Amplify disability advocates and partners — quote-tweet/repost with added context
- Respond to relevant news within hours, not days
- Never be defensive about the "industry front" criticism — be transparent
- Engage respectfully with skeptics, especially disability community voices
- Don't argue with bad-faith criticism — state position once and move on
