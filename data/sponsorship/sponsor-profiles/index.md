## Profile Template

Each sponsor profile should include:

1. **Company Overview** — Relevant business context
2. **Accessibility/AI Programs** — Existing investments we align with
3. **Alignment Analysis** — Why AI-AA fits their strategy
4. **Products as Comprehension Aids** — Specific products that function as auxiliary aids
5. **Entry Points** — Teams, people, programs to approach
6. **Budget Alignment** — Which existing budget line funds us
7. **What They Get** — Customized value proposition
8. **Risks & Objections** — Anticipated concerns and responses
9. **Outreach History** — Every interaction logged with dates
10. **Current Status** — Pipeline stage and next action
