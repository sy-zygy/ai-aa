## Principles

1. **CEO sends the first email.** C-suite contacts require peer-level outreach.
2. **No money in the first email.** Ask for 20 minutes, not $500K.
3. **Reference their data, not ours.** Show we've done our homework.
4. **One warm intro is worth 50 cold emails.** Prioritize bridge contacts (Be My Eyes CEO Mike Buckley is highest-value).
5. **Follow up within 1 week.** If no response in 2 weeks, activate alternate path.
