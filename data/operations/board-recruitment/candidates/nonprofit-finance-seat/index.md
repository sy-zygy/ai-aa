# Nonprofit Finance Seat — Sourcing Strategy

**Stage:** Sourcing | **Seat Category:** Nonprofit Governance Expert | **Priority:** Medium (lower urgency than AT practitioner — financial governance can be addressed through committee structure in interim)

## What We're Looking For

A board member with nonprofit financial governance expertise who can:
- Chair or serve on the Finance Committee
- Oversee annual budgets, financial reporting, and audit processes
- Ensure compliance with IRS requirements (Form 990, intermediate sanctions)
- Guide fiscal policies as the organization scales from near-zero to grant-funded operations
- Provide financial oversight credibility that sponsors and grantors expect

**Ideal profile:**
- CPA, nonprofit CFO/controller, or experienced nonprofit treasurer
- Experience with 501(c)(3) startup financials (not just mature-org accounting)
- Familiarity with fiscal sponsorship transitions
- Bonus: Connection to disability rights or social justice nonprofit sector
- No conflicts with AI-AA sponsors

- The Board can establish a Finance Committee with external advisors (non-board members with financial expertise)
