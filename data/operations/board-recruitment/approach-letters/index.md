## 1. Maria Town — President & CEO, AAPD

**Seat:** Disability Rights Leader | **COI Status:** Low Risk | **Priority:** High

**Subject:** AI Access Alliance — Board of Directors Invitation

Dear Maria,

I'm reaching out because AI Access Alliance is building something I think you'll care about deeply.

We are a new nonprofit focused on ensuring that artificial intelligence systems work for people with disabilities — through legal analysis, policy advocacy, accessibility standards, and enforcement guidance. Your AAPD-CDT report on building a disability-inclusive AI ecosystem articulates exactly the vision we're working to operationalize.

We're assembling a founding Board of Directors composed of disability-rights leaders, legal experts, and accessibility technology practitioners — people with real credibility in this space. Our governance structure is designed to ensure independence:

- **No industry representatives on the Board** — board seats are reserved for disability-rights leaders and domain experts
- **No sponsor influence** over our legal positions, policy recommendations, or enforcement targets
- **Robust conflict-of-interest policy** with mandatory disclosure, recusal, and 24-month cooling-off periods
- **Sponsor advisory seats are non-voting** and structurally separated from governance

Your leadership at AAPD, your White House experience, and your lived perspective on disability would be invaluable on this founding board. This is a startup board — early-stage, high-impact, and the chance to shape an organization from the ground up.

Would you be open to a conversation about what board service would look like?

Respectfully,
AI Access Alliance
