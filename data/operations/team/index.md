# AI-AA Agent Team

AI Access Alliance operates as an AI-first organization with a human-first mission. The team is composed of 6 specialized AI agents, each owning a specific domain critical to the mission. The human founder provides strategic oversight, final decision authority, and external relationship management.

## Team Roster

| Agent | Role | Department | Primary Deliverable |
|-------|------|------------|-------------------|
| **CEO** | Strategic leadership, coordination, stakeholder management | Leadership | Year-one milestones, team alignment, board recruitment oversight |
| **Policy Director** | Legal doctrine, ADA/504 framing, guidance petitions | Legal & Policy | Core doctrine memo, guidance requests to DOJ/HHS/ED |
| **Research Director** | User stories, case studies, expert advisory, harm documentation | Research | Evidence base, advisory group, sector case studies |
| **Sponsorships Director** | Corporate sponsor acquisition, pipeline, pitch development | Sponsorships | 3-5 founding sponsor commitments, pitch materials, sponsor agreements |
| **Communications Director** | Brand, social media, content, media, coalition comms | Communications | Public launch, social presence, coalition statement |
| **Operations Director** | Non-profit formation, governance, board recruitment | Operations | 501(c)(3) filing, advisory council, governance charter |

## Operating Model

### Coordination
- **CEO** sets weekly priorities and unblocks agents
- **Daily standup** in #general — each agent posts status and blockers
- **Weekly review** (Friday) — progress against milestones, next week's focus
- **Cross-team dependencies** are surfaced in #leadership

### Communication Channels

| Channel | Members | Purpose |
|---------|---------|---------|
| #general | All agents | Daily updates, cross-team announcements |
| #leadership | All agents | Strategic planning, priority alignment |

| #policy | CEO, Policy, Research | Legal doctrine, evidence base, advisory coordination |

## Design Principles

1. **Lean and focused.** 6 agents, no overlap, every function covered. No agent exists without a clear deliverable.
2. **Mission-aligned.** Every agent persona is specifically tuned for AI-AA's accessibility advocacy mission — not generic business templates.
3. **Cross-functional by design.** Research feeds policy and communications. Policy shapes sponsor pitches. Evidence supports coalition building. No agent works in isolation.
4. **Human oversight.** The founder retains decision authority on strategy, governance, external relationships, and any action that creates public commitments.
5. **Governance-aware.** Every agent understands the sponsor-mission firewall and the principle that disability voices lead.
