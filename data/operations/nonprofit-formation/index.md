# Non-Profit Formation

**Owner:** Operations Director | **Target:** 501(c)(3) filing by Q2 2026

Tracker for standing up AI Access Alliance as a legal non-profit entity.

## Entity Decision

**Chosen structure:** 501(c)(3) now, with future 501(c)(4) affiliate if lobbying becomes necessary.

**Rationale:**
- 501(c)(3) allows tax-deductible donations — critical for corporate sponsors and foundation grants
- Limited lobbying is permitted under 501(h) election (up to specified spending limits)
- Most disability-rights organizations use this dual structure
- 501(c)(4) affiliate can be added later when direct lobbying becomes a strategic priority

## Form 1023 vs. 1023-EZ

| Criteria | 1023-EZ | Full 1023 |
|----------|---------|-----------|
| Projected annual gross receipts | Under $50K for 3 years | Over $50K |
| Total assets | Under $250K | Over $250K |
| Processing time | ~2-4 weeks | ~3-6 months |
| Filing fee | $275 | $600 |
| Complexity | 3-page form | 28+ pages |

**Decision needed:** If we expect founding sponsors in the $500K+ range within the first year, the full Form 1023 is likely required. Operations Director to confirm with legal counsel.
