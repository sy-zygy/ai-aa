## Parallel Path — Don't Wait for Counsel

The Articles of Incorporation draft is based on standard Delaware nonprofit language and IRS Publication 557 templates. The blockers are:

1. **Registered agent** — Can be selected without counsel ($100-150/year, commercial service)
2. **Initial directors** — Need founder name + 2 early-committed board members
3. **Incorporator** — CEO decision (founder or attorney?)

**We can resolve blockers 1-3 and file Articles while counsel engagement is in progress.** Delaware Articles for a basic nonprofit are well-templated and low-risk. Legal review of the Form 1023 is where counsel is most critical — and we have weeks to establish that relationship before Form 1023 is ready.
