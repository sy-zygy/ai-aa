## The Sponsor Governance Firewall

The single most important governance provision. Must include:

- Sponsors receive **no board votes**
- Sponsors have **no veto** over legal positions or policy recommendations
- Sponsors get **no access** to pre-publication policy drafts
- Sponsors have **no influence** over enforcement targets or litigation decisions
- All sponsor agreements **expressly state** these restrictions
- Sponsor advisory seats are **non-voting** and clearly separated from governance
- Sponsor representatives on the advisory council **must recuse** from discussions where their company's interests are directly at stake
