# Board Member Agreement

**Status:** DRAFT v0.1 | **Last Updated:** 2026-04-06 | **Owner:** Operations Director

*This agreement is signed by each member of the Board of Directors upon appointment. It establishes mutual expectations between the Director and AI Access Alliance.*

---

## Agreement

I, _________________________ ("Director"), accept appointment to the Board of Directors of AI Access Alliance ("the Organization") and agree to the following terms for my service.

---

## 1. Mission Commitment

I understand that AI Access Alliance exists to advance equitable access to artificial intelligence for people with disabilities and neurodivergent individuals. I commit to acting in furtherance of this mission in all Board-related decisions and activities.

I affirm that the perspectives of disabled and neurodivergent people must be centered in the Organization's work — consistent with the principle of "nothing about us without us."

---

## 2. Fiduciary Duties

As a Director, I accept the following fiduciary duties under law:

### 2.1 — Duty of Care
I will exercise reasonable care in my oversight of the Organization, including:
- Preparing for and attending Board meetings
- Reviewing financial statements and organizational reports
- Asking informed questions and exercising independent judgment
- Making decisions in the best interests of the Organization

### 2.2 — Duty of Loyalty
I will put the interests of the Organization ahead of my personal interests, including:
- Disclosing all conflicts of interest promptly and fully
- Recusing myself from decisions where I have a conflict
- Not using my position for personal gain or the gain of any affiliated entity
- Maintaining confidentiality of Board deliberations and non-public information

### 2.3 — Duty of Obedience
I will ensure that the Organization operates in accordance with:
- Its Articles of Incorporation and Bylaws
- Applicable federal and state laws
- Its tax-exempt purpose under Section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code
- Its adopted governance policies, including the Sponsor Governance Policy and Conflict of Interest Policy

---

## 3. Time Commitment

I understand the expected time commitment and agree to make reasonable efforts to fulfill it:

| Activity | Frequency | Estimated Time |
|----------|-----------|---------------|
| Board meetings | Quarterly (minimum 4/year) | 2-3 hours per meeting |
| Meeting preparation | Before each meeting | 1-2 hours per meeting |
| Annual meeting | Once per year | Half-day to full-day |
| Committee service | Per committee schedule | 1-2 hours/month |
| Ad hoc consultations | As needed | Variable |
| **Total estimated annual commitment** | | **40-60 hours/year** |

I understand that the initial formation period (Year 1) may require additional time as the Organization establishes its programs and governance.

---

## 4. Attendance

I will attend at least seventy-five percent (75%) of regular Board meetings per calendar year. If I am unable to attend a meeting, I will notify the Board Chair in advance and review the materials and minutes.

I understand that a Director who misses three (3) consecutive meetings without prior notice or good cause may be asked to resign or be subject to removal per the Bylaws.

---

## 5. Governance Policies

I have received, read, and agree to comply with the following governance documents:

- [ ] Bylaws of AI Access Alliance
- [ ] Sponsor Governance Policy
- [ ] Conflict of Interest Policy
- [ ] Whistleblower Policy (when adopted)
- [ ] Document Retention Policy (when adopted)

I understand that these policies may be amended by the Board and that I will be bound by amendments adopted in accordance with the Bylaws.

---

## 6. Sponsor Independence

I understand and affirm the Organization's structural separation between corporate sponsorship and governance. Specifically:

- I am not a current employee, officer, director, or paid consultant of any AI-AA corporate sponsor
- I do not hold any financial interest that would create a conflict with the Organization's mission
- I will comply with the Sponsor Governance Policy and will not facilitate, directly or indirectly, any sponsor influence over the Organization's policy positions, legal filings, research publications, or enforcement decisions
- If my employment or financial interests change in a way that could create a conflict, I will disclose the change to the Board Chair immediately

---

## 7. Conflict of Interest

I will complete the Annual Conflict of Interest Disclosure and Certification:
- Upon signing this agreement
- Annually thereafter, no later than January 31
- Upon any material change in circumstances

I understand that failure to disclose a conflict of interest may result in removal from the Board.

---

## 8. Confidentiality

I will maintain the confidentiality of:
- Board deliberations and discussions not designated for public disclosure
- Draft policy positions, legal strategies, and research findings prior to publication
- Personnel matters, financial details, and donor/sponsor information not publicly disclosed
- Any information designated as confidential by the Board or Executive Director

This obligation survives my departure from the Board.

---

## 9. Public Representation

I understand that:
- Only designated spokespersons may make public statements on behalf of AI-AA
- I may publicly identify myself as a Board member of AI-AA
- I may not imply that my personal views represent the Organization's positions without authorization
- Media inquiries about AI-AA should be directed to the Communications Director or Executive Director

---

## 10. Compensation

I understand that:
- Board service is uncompensated
- I may be reimbursed for reasonable expenses incurred in the performance of Board duties (travel, accommodation for meetings), subject to Treasurer approval
- Reimbursement requests must include documentation and be submitted within thirty (30) days

---

## 11. Term and Departure

- My term is _____ year(s), beginning on ____________ and ending on ____________
- I may resign at any time by submitting written notice to the Board Chair
- I may be removed by a two-thirds (2/3) vote of the Board per the Bylaws
- Upon departure, I will return all Organization materials, documents, and property
- My confidentiality obligations survive my departure

---

## 12. Annual Certification

I will annually review and re-affirm this agreement. The Governance Committee will circulate the annual re-affirmation alongside the Conflict of Interest certification each January.
